Destiny 2: The Story

The Story of Destiny takes places thousands of years into our future, and across our solar system. About 700 years into our future, humanity discovered the first signs of extraterrestrial intelligence, in the form of what was called " "The Traveler". A Terraforming intelligence which humanity first met while it was terraforming the martian landscape, after The Traveler was observed terraforming a Jovian moon.

What happened?

Meeting the Traveler ushered in what was known as "Humanity's Golden Age" in this era humanity advanced in a short spam of time what it hadn't advance for thousands of years. The traveler tripled humanity's lifespan, and helped humanity develop technologies which allowed humanity inhabit almost all of the solar system. Cities were build around every planet or moon the traveler touched." And for about one Thousand years humanity grew and expanded, but this was not to last. With Humanities advancement, other intelligence came after the traveler. This intelligence was known as "The Darkness". Contrasting the Light and Advancement brought about by The traveler, The Darkness brought with it rapid destruction, and with The Darkness others civilizations followed in search of the technology and resources developed by The Traveler on our Solar system. This period is known as "The Collapse".

Enter: The Guardian

However, what was left of humanity was defenseless again the looming threat, as it was dying, the traveler created miniature copies of itself, now known as Ghosts, and this allowed for selected group of beings to be resurrected, receive immortality and the strength to fight for humanity. These beings are known as Guardians. The battle between the Darkness and The Traveler resulted in a stalemate. This stalemate was broken when the Traveler, sacrifice itself and in doing so expelled the darkness from the system. this left humanity defenseless against the other alien forces still roaming the solar system.

And just when humanity needs you the most, a ghost, your Ghost, finds YOU, the player and wakes you up from death to fight for the good of humanity, as a "Guardian of the Light" , or simply a "Guardian".

"Eyes up, Guardian!" Your Ghost, upon resurrecting you as the "Guardian"!
